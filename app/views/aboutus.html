More about us

Nowadays, more and more people need help while fewer and fewer can afford to give. Based on this observation, the goal of Givago is to propose an alternative form of grants, different from the traditional donation system such as giving money or volunteering. With Givago, by only taking a few seconds of your time, you can support causes that care for you without spending any money! You can make a concrete action to turn the world into a better place.
The current version of Givago is only the 1st step of a long journey!
Our goal is to enhance a better world with the power of Internet.
