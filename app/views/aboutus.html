About us

Nowadays more and more people need help whilst fewer and fewer can afford to give. Based on this observation Givago proposes an alternative way to donate to charity, different from the traditional (out-dated) donation system we have in place today. With Givago, by only taking a few seconds of your time (and watching a 30 seconds advertisement clip, or downloading an app from our sponsors), you can support causes you care for without spending any money!

This is the first step of a long journey. Our goal is you use the power of advertisements on the Internet to make the world a better place and with your help we can do so!

Givago as it stands allows you to give to charity without breaking the bank. In the future, it will enable you to create your own local and global projects with the help of our global Givago community.
